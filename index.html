Research Interests

Fueled by flight mechanics, mathematics, bio-inspiration, and a passion for aerospace engineering, our research is geared towards safely and efficiently completing flight missions and tackling associated GNC challenges. We design innovative aerial systems and techniques to achieve autonomous guidance and control, boost safety, and advance aerial mobility - realizing the transition from inspiration to operation.

  • Flight Mechanics
  • Trajectory Optimization
  • Bio-inspired Flight Techniques
  • Guidance, Navigation, and Control
  • Electric Aircraft
